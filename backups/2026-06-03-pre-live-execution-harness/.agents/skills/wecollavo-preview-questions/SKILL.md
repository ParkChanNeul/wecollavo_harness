# WeCollavo Preview Questions

Use this skill before or during a WeCollavo client meeting.

Read `docs/source-of-truth.md`, `docs/tracks.md`, `docs/live-cockpit.md`,
`docs/workflow.md`, and the client snapshot first.

## Output

Create or update `harness/workspaces/<client-slug>/preview-questions.md`.

Use three groups:

1. **Discovery Questions**
   - What is the current visible problem?
   - What asset, page, profile, logo, or content is not usable yet?
   - Where will the output be used: sales, partnership, public-sector,
     investor, hiring, or homepage?
   - Who decides final approval?
   - What materials already exist?

2. **Decision Questions**
   - What must be finished first for visible business value?
   - What is the acceptable first-phase budget?
   - What deadline matters and why?
   - What is excluded from this phase?
   - What revision rule and additional-fee rule will protect the schedule?

3. **Follow-up And Risk Questions**
   - Which answers are vague, contradictory, or price-sensitive?
   - Which client materials are missing?
   - Which claims need proof before public use?
   - Which expectation could become a late revision or payment risk?

Keep questions plain enough for a non-marketing owner to answer.
