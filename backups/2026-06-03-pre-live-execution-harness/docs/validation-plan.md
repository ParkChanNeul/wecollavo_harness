## Scope

This is not a software test plan yet. It is a business/process validation plan for the
Brand Execution Sprint and the two-track WeCollavo offer.

## Validation Matrix

| Flow | Happy Path | Failure Path | Edge Case | Evidence |
|------|------------|--------------|-----------|----------|
| Free diagnosis | Lead answers track-specific questions and accepts next step | Lead wants guaranteed immediate revenue | Lead fits both tracks | Completed diagnosis notes, accepted/rejected status |
| Live Cockpit | AI backchannel improves the next question during the meeting | Backchannel is shown to the client or treated as final judgment | Customer asks for price before materials are available | Live notes, backchannel, proposal snapshot |
| Anti-target filter | Bad-fit lead is declined or redirected | Bad-fit lead is accepted and scope drifts | Existing relationship makes refusal hard | Rejection reason logged |
| Linchpin Scorecard | Score guides offer and scope | Score is filled after the decision to justify it | Borderline 36-47 score | Scorecard attached to estimate |
| Media Funnel Map | Customer understands interest/trust/authority/conversion flow | Map becomes generic content advice | Customer has no existing audience/channel | Map included in delivery |
| Core asset production | One asset ships with use case and next action | Asset expands beyond paid scope | Customer requests logo + landing + brochure in base scope | Signed scope and change-order notes |
| Proof Loop | 30-day signal is collected | No follow-up happens | Customer uses asset offline only | Before/after/30-day record |
| Retainer bridge | Next 30 Days plan makes monthly operation natural | Retainer feels like forced upsell | Customer wants execution but not monthly fee | Retainer proposal outcome |

## Must-Test Scenarios

1. Track 1 lead with urgent brochure/homepage need accepts page-based pricing.
2. Track 1 lead tries to include full rebrand or new logo in base scope.
3. Track 2 lead wants a logo only, but has no message, target, or sales channel.
4. Track 2 lead has strong audience but unclear independent product promise.
5. Existing client is a bad fit by Anti-Target Filter but relationship pressure exists.
6. Proof Loop is impossible to collect because the customer does not deploy the asset.
7. Live meeting produces a field diagnostic proposal, but final estimate must wait for materials.

## Confidence Gate

Do not scale the offer until these are true:

- 3 paid sprint attempts completed or rejected with documented reasons.
- At least 1 Proof Loop collected 30 days after delivery.
- At least 1 estimate used a track-specific price structure.
- At least 1 live meeting used `live-meeting.md`, `ai-backchannel.md`, and `proposal-snapshot.md`.
- At least 1 lead was declined or scoped down using the Anti-Target Filter.
- Track 1 and Track 2 sales materials are separate.
