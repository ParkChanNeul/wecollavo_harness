# Human Linchpin Review

Run this before sending any client-facing presentation, estimate, or proposal.

## Judgment

- The conclusion matches the client's real bottleneck, not only the stated ask.
- The recommendation fits Track 1 or Track 2 without mixing offers.
- The first phase is small enough to execute and valuable enough to sell.
- The client can understand the next action without marketing knowledge.

## Price And Scope

- The price table explains what is included.
- Exclusions are visible.
- Additional-fee triggers are visible.
- Revision limits are clear.
- Client material dependencies are clear.
- The payment timing and delivery timing are realistic.

## Risk

- No public claims are invented.
- No delivery guarantee is overstated.
- No unlimited revision promise is implied.
- No client confidentiality issue is exposed.
- Late-payment, late-material, and late-revision risks are addressed.

## Delivery

- `client-presentation.html` opens in a browser.
- The first screen shows the conclusion.
- The "why" section is specific to the client.
- Pricing is detailed enough for a representative to react to.
- The final wording is safe to send.
