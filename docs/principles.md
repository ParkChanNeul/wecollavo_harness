# Operating Principles

1. AI is a scribe, advisor, risk detector, and document builder. It is not the
   client-facing consultant.
2. Channeul is the Linchpin who translates internal judgment into client-safe
   language.
3. The harness must reduce meeting-time cognitive load.
4. The harness must protect scope, margin, payment, and timeline.
5. A proposal is not finished until it can become a delivery plan.
6. Internal notes and domain-agent outputs are never sent directly to the client.
7. Proof Loop evidence is part of the product, not a nice-to-have.
