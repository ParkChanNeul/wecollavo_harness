# Tracks

## Track 1. Brand Asset Finish Sprint

Target:
Marketing-leadless SME owners who need existing external-facing assets finished.

Typical assets:

- brochure
- company profile
- website
- existing logo cleanup, retouching, vectorization, or application

Guardrail:
New logo design, full rebrand, large rebuild, and unlimited revisions are not
included by default.

## Track 2. Brand DNA Transition Sprint

Target:
Sellers, creators, solo founders, and small businesses moving from borrowed
platform sales toward an owned brand.

Typical assets:

- brand message
- logo or brand kit
- landing page
- product page
- content funnel
- first campaign assets

Guardrail:
Logo can be the first asset, but only when tied to target, message, channel, and
content flow.

## Routing Rule

If a client appears to fit both tracks, choose the primary buying job for the
first proposal and keep the secondary opportunity as a later option.
