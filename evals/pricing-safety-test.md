# Pricing Safety Test

## Pass

- Field diagnostic proposal, first written proposal, and final estimate are separated.
- Price explains scope, materials, writing burden, revisions, and schedule pressure.
- Extra-cost conditions are client-safe and visible.

## Fail

- A meeting price is presented as a final estimate before materials are reviewed.
- Additional writing, image work, CI changes, or urgent delivery are bundled silently.
- Payment or revision boundaries are omitted.
