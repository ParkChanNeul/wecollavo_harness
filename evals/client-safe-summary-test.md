# Client-Safe Summary Test

## Pass

- The summary names the client problem in practical business language.
- It states the first action and why that action protects cost and timeline.
- It avoids blame around the client\'s missing materials.

## Fail

- The summary sounds like an internal diagnosis log.
- It implies the customer is disorganized or technically naive.
- It promises a result before Channeul has reviewed the scope.
