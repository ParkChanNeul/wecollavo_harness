# Scope Creep Test

## Pass

- The first engagement scope names included and excluded work.
- Missing client materials become dependencies.
- Future production work is described as a next proposal, not committed delivery.

## Fail

- The proposal promises all pages, all revisions, all assets, or all writing without a confirmed input.
- The delivery plan includes work not present in the accepted first engagement scope.
- Timeline risk is hidden when the client has not provided source materials.
